Density of Airbnb properties in Mallorca

  • Module: https://github.com/Leaflet/Leaflet.heat/
  • Data source: Inside Airbnb licensed by CC 3.0 BY.
  • Processing: the coordinates of each property are extracted from the CSV file provided by Inside Airbnb.

The Bay of Palma (Bahía de Palma) is almost totally covered while the Tramuntana is relatively saved. The density in the Alcudia-Pollença area (north of the island) is also highlighted.
